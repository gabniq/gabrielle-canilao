About Me!

Art has been something that I have wielded all my life, inspired from Saturday morning cartoons, where I stood amazed, aiming to create something just as beautiful. As such, drawing has been an essential point of self for as long as I can remember, and have created digital illustrations for a decade. With experience in digital illustration, I have also delved into graphic design, creating logos or business cards for clients. Additionally, while graphic design is not something that I often do, I do still create commissioned digital illustrations for clients, from portraiture to character designs.

In my art practice, while it primarily started with my experiences in digital illustration, I have always been fond of experimentation, looking into new styles, techniques, and even mediums. Thus, my practice is an overlap of things I simply enjoy, from collage, to video, to game design. In creating art, I set out not with limits nor an exact goal, but a flow to explore follow in order to create something that I can be proud of, no matter how far from my original idea it is. It is a process of trial and error, of trying things that may or may not work out, and not settling for something that I cannot proudly show.

Overall, while my works often fluctuate in both subject and style, I always imbue a little piece of myself in each project. One such hobby is that of music, as I am someone who always has their headphones in, listening to music at any given moment. Much like my art practice, my taste in music is just as varied and experimental. From K-pop to Rock to Classical, there is no genre that I will not touch, and these various songs are what drive me to create, a perfect motivation for art. Additionally, fashion has always been a major part of my sense of self, another way for me to express myself besides my art. Thus, I take the styles that inspire me and put it into my art, emphasizing the stylistic choices in both art and fashion within my pieces. It is my belief that, more than anything else about a person, their wardrobe choices are the best representation of who they are.

In terms of goals, while my practice is widely varied, my most well-loved mediums have always been digital illustration and video game development. As mentioned before, the beauty in what digital art I saw as a child is what inspired me to begin it in the first place, and it has always stuck with me. As for video games, it is a much newer medium for me; however, I cannot help but be enraptured every time that I play a game, amazed by even the smallest details. Thus, I strive to create games myself, combining it with my digital illustrations to create universes that completely take the player somewhere new.
