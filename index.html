Glitch Art ⋆⭒˚.⋆

For my glitch album project, I chose to create a new cover for Radiohead's In Rainbows, one of my favorite albums of all time. The three images on the top are the original glitched versions of possible album covers. In order to represent the experimental feel of the album, I aimed to achieve some experimental glitches myself, enhancing the colors and trying to achieve interesting compositions. In the final album covers on the bottom (with the right hand one being a plastic-wrapped version with a barcode), I continued experimenting with layer blend modes on Photoshop and chose to isolate a subject to draw the eye in.

₊‧.°.⋆ Vector Sticker Art ⋆.°.‧₊

The left image above is a vectorized drawing of a figure that I had made in Illustrator. Afterward, I took the final drawing into Medibang, an art app I use on my iPad, to create a border around it and convert it into a sticker. Using masking, textures, and blend modes, I projected it onto an image of my guitar, as well as some other stickers added to make it look more natural.

₊‧.°.⋆ Creative Coding Portrait⋆.°.‧₊

This portrait was made in p5.js using Javascript. First, I quickly sketched the image using various shape tools on Medibang. Next, I created a grid to overlay on it, in order to determine the vertex points I would need when coding. The animation of the portrait involves interpolation and changes the color of the shines.
